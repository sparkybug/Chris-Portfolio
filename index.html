Products I have worked on

What I can help you with

I can help you design your company or personal website, and mobile application.

As a designer, I pride myself in delivering designs that are of high standard, increasing user engagement and overall productivity.

I follow a standard process in delivering a world class design, from user research which is a very important part in the design process, to defining a problem, ideating, design and test. I am very meticulous about these stages.

Explore my designs

What stand me out

As a user experience designer, I strongly believe in advocating for users as well as the business - a balance which I feel must be struck.

I follow a standard process in delivering a world class design, from user research which is very important in the design process, to defining a problem, ideating, design and test. I am very meticulous about these stages

I am very patient with my clients and open to as many revisions needed by my clients, with the sole aim of satisfying them, meeting business requirements and solving user's problem.
